Bree Frances is a singer-songwriter with a passion for storytelling and emotional connection. She began making music at 14 after a dance injury pushed her to find a new creative outlet and she hasn’t stopped since. Known for her distinct vocal tone and heartfelt lyricism, Bree is dedicated to telling stories that often go unheard and exploring emotions that aren’t always discussed. Through her music, she aims to create a sense of belonging, making listeners feel seen and understood. She believes that the best music comes from honesty, and she strives to stay true to herself in every song she writes.
